Tail

Kyvera have distinctly unique and complex tails. Not much is understood about why their tails are the way they are but the purpose of some of the tail's traits was discovered recently.


They seem to be able to manipulate the length of their tails.

Armora

"Armora" is the name given to the plates covering the tail of the kyvera. These plates seem to play a role in how the kyvera is able to manipulate the length of their and how the species is able to stand on their tails.
